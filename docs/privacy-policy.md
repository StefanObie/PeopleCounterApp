# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 10, 2026

People Counter ("the App") is designed to work offline and process images on your device.

## Summary

- We do not collect personal data.
- We do not upload your photos or detection results to any server.
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
- All image analysis is performed locally on your device.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information from users.

The App does not require account creation and does not include user analytics, advertising SDKs, or cloud-based image processing.

## How the App Uses Data

When you select or capture an image, the App processes that image on-device to detect and count people.

- Processing happens entirely on your device.
- Images are not sent to the developer or any external servers.
- Detection results remain on your device unless you choose to share content yourself using your device tools.

## Permissions

Depending on your device and Android version, the App may request access to:

- **Camera**: to capture images for counting.
- **Photos/Media/Storage**: to select images from your gallery.

These permissions are used only for the features you choose to use.

## Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties.

## Data Retention

Because we do not collect user data on servers, there is no server-side retention of your personal data.

Any images or results you keep are stored locally on your device under your control.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Security

Since processing is local and offline, your images are not transmitted over the internet by the App.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in this repository, and the "Effective date" above will be updated.
